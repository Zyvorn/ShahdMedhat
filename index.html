About Me

From Teen Spirit to Wise Words is exactly what you will explore with Shahd Medhat, a female Bilingual Egyptian Voiceover Artist fluent in Arabic (Egyptian), Standard Modern Arabic, and English. As a bilingual voice-over talent, Shahd is fully responsive and ensures top-quality delivery and recordings. Clients can count on Shahd as a dependable partner in their process.

Shahd's versatility shines through in her ability to tackle different styles, from warm and authentic to energetic and sarcastic. Her naturally energetic voice can smoothly transition from teenager to young adult. Whether you need a commercial voice-over, video narrator, friendly IVR voice, etc... Shahd's voice is Bridging the Gap Between Brands and Target audiences.

Shahd aims to create something Remarkable! ensuring every detail is perfect. In addition to her voice-over talent, Shahd is also skilled in audio engineering. She can edit, process, and mix tracks to ensure your project sounds polished and professional.
