Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Why should I choose DRAVIX to build my website?

DRAVIX specializes in creating high-quality, performance-optimized websites with modern UI/UX design. We focus exclusively on front-end development, which allows us to deliver pixel-perfect, responsive websites that load quickly and provide exceptional user experiences.

What technologies do you use for front-end development?

We use the latest front-end technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), and modern frameworks like React.js and Vue.js. We also work with CSS preprocessors (Sass, Less) and build tools to ensure optimal performance and maintainability.

Can you make my site mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach. We ensure your site looks and functions perfectly on all devices, from smartphones to tablets and desktops, providing an optimal user experience regardless of screen size.

Do you offer custom designs or use templates?

We specialize in custom designs tailored to your brand and business needs. While we can work with templates if requested, we typically create unique designs that help your business stand out from competitors.

How fast can I get my site done?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but most standard websites take 2-4 weeks from concept to launch. We provide a detailed project timeline during our initial consultation and work efficiently to deliver on schedule without compromising quality.

Can I contact you directly via WhatsApp or Facebook?

Yes, we're available on both WhatsApp (+20 109 626 8248) and Facebook. Feel free to message us with your inquiries at any time, and we'll respond as quickly as possible during our business hours.

Do you support Arabic and English content on the site?

Yes, we offer full bilingual support for both Arabic and English. Our websites are designed with RTL (Right-to-Left) compatibility for Arabic content and seamless language switching functionality.
